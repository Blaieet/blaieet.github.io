About me

I was born in Barcelona on March 15, 1997. During high school I developed my first computer skills, publishing an Android app on 11th grade. I started a degree on Computer Science in 2015 at the Universitat de Barcelona (UB), graduating 5 years later in February 2020. During my career I worked at Andro4all, SEAT, Eurecat, Accenture and Immfly.

After these years I developed a passion for Data Science, starting, therefore, the "Fundamental principles of Data Science" master at the Universitat de Barcelona, while working for Accenture. I obtained my title in 2021.

Since 2022, I'm also a professor at Universitat de Barcelona, where I teach data-related subjects, guiding future computer scientists with industrial expertise in machine learning, data science and software development. I also try to advocate always the use of Linux and open-source technologies, fundamental of everything we've accomplished today.

If we enter in a more personal space, I always played football ⚽, loved pizza 🍕 and sometimes I skate, snowboard & swim 🏊. I'm also getting better at ping-pong 🏓. I enjoy sunny days, taking care of my plants 🪴 and photography, both digital and analog.

You can always email me or contact me on my socials, like Instagram or LinkedIn.
